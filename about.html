My name is J.V and I was born and raised in Detroit, MI. I have many interests and hobbies but music is my bread and butter (shouts-out to Motown). Many friends and acquaintances have asked me what I don't do after meeting them. I like creating no matter what it is and have always had a creative mind. I am currently studying Computer Science at Michigan State University and have plans to one day start my own business. I plan to bring all of my skills and talents together beyond graduation to introduce something new to the world.

When not studying, I am locked in on my Digital Audio Workstation, FL Studio, producing music and working on my own sound. The creativity never stops and neither does the work. Every so often, I take a trip outside and ride my skateboard. I'm probably too old to become a pro-skater, but I enjoy reaching milestones with every one of my endeavors. This being said, after almost 10 years of "on-and-off" skateboarding, I can land a kickflip with one foot. On top of this physical activity, I also like to run and work out.
